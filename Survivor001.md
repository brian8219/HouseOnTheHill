Haunt 01 Survivor
# 木乃伊橫行
_塵土刮進房間，一絲不安閃過你的腦中。你聽見一個朋友放聲大叫，狂起且嚇人。一個冷冽沁骨聲音，顫抖的傳入進你的耳裡：「我失去我的新娘，比你所能想像的時間還要久。我的淚堆積成沙，但我的愛依舊如艷陽般熾熱。我的愛人，如今，為我重生。再也沒有認和是能將我們分開，如果你想與我為敵，我會將你的靈魂，從你的身體，生吞活剝。」_

## 此刻
將兩個三角形知識檢定標記物放到一旁備用。
叛徒失去女孩和它所帶來的功效。取而代之，他放置一個小洋紅色怪物標記物（代表女孩），在與作祟被揭露的房間同一層的任何一個房間裡。並且至少離木乃伊5格遠。如果那一層沒有任何一間房間離木乃伊5格遠，則他盡可能將標記物放到該層最遠的房間。
當一位探險者進到女孩指示物所在的房間時，該玩家獲得女孩。

## 關於壞人你知道
叛徒試圖讓木乃伊和女孩結婚。

## 你獲勝於⋯⋯
⋯⋯在木乃伊和女孩結婚前，將祂驅逐回亡者的國度。

## 如何驅逐木乃伊
如果書還沒有在場上，下一個探索到有預兆符號房間的探險者，可以直接從預兆卡堆中找出書，而不是像平常那樣隨機抽卡。然後將卡堆重新洗牌。
英雄必須念出記載在書裡的木乃伊真名，來將祂驅逐回亡者的國度。你必須按照順序執行下面這些步驟。每位英雄一回合只能執行一個步驟。
1. 在下列其中一間房間裡，擲出知識檢定6+以找出木乃伊的真名：
    - 放置**石棺**的房間（研究象形文字）、
    - **研究室**（閱讀考古團隊的筆記），或
    - **圖書室**（研究木乃伊的歷史）。
如果你成功，拿取一個知識檢定指示物。
2. 在英雄發現木乃伊真名之後的回合裡，如果你擁有書，你可以嘗試知識檢定6+來查尋木乃伊的名字並學習驅逐祂的咒語。如果你成功，拿取一個知識檢定指示物。
3. 當英雄方擁有兩個知識檢定指示物時，其中一位英雄必須帶著書到木乃伊所在的房間。任何一位與書和木乃伊同房間的英雄，可以嘗試用神智和木乃伊戰鬥，擊敗並且完成將祂永遠驅逐的咒語。

木乃伊對速度攻擊免疫（例如左輪手槍和炸藥）

## 如果你獲勝⋯⋯
_你用力地關上上古墓的門。一陣溫熱，乾燥的風，吹過房間。木乃伊蹣跚向你。空洞的眼神填滿絕望。祂的手向你的脖子靠近，上頭的繃帶開始瓦解。你眼前怪物的身體隨著熱風消散，哀嚎著：「我的新娘……我的摯愛……再也……不……」
風，隨著最後一片殘塊的消失而停止。留你獨自一人。_

指示物：木乃伊（Mummy）
房間：研究室（Research Laboratory）、圖書室（Library）
預兆：女孩（Girl）、書（Book）
物品：左輪手槍（Revolver）、炸藥（Dynamite）

The Mummy Walks
Dust billows into the room and a shadow passes over your heart. You hear one of your friends scream, a sound of delight and horror. A cold, damp voice shivers in your mind. “I lost my bride more years ago than you could possibly comprehend. My tears are dust, but my love is still as strong as the sun. Now my love is reborn to me. There is nothing you can do to keep us apart-and if you turn against me, I will rip your soul from your body and swallow it whole.”

Right Now
Set aside 2 triangular Knowledge Roll tokens.
The traitor loses the Girl card and any bonuses from it. Instead, he or she puts a small magenta monster token (representing the Girl) in any room on the same floor as the room where the haunt was revealed and at least 5 tiles away from the Mummy. If no rooms are at least 5 tiles away, he or she places the token as far away as possible on that floor.
When an explorer enters the same room as the Girl token, that player takes Girl card.

What You Know About the Bad Guys
The traitor is trying to get Mummy married to the Girl.

You Win When…
… you banish the Mummy back to the land of the dead before it marries the Girl.

How to Banish the Mummy
If the Book is not yet in play, the next hero to discover a room with an omen symbol searches through the omen stack and takes the Book card instead of drawing normally. Then shuffle that stack.
A hero must speak the Mummy’s true name from the Book to banish it. You must follow these steps, in order, to do this. Each hero can attempt only one step each turn.
Attempt a Knowledge roll of 6+ to discover the Mummy’s true name in one of the following rooms:
the room with the Sarcophagus (studying hieroglyphics);
the Research Laboratory (scanning the archaeological team’s notes); or
the Library (researching the Mummy’s history).
If you succeed, take a Knowledge Roll token.
On a turn after any hero discover the name, if you possess the Book, you can attempt a Knowledge roll of 6+ to look up the Mummy’s name and learn the spell that will banish it. If you succeed, take a Knowledge Roll token.
Once the heroes have two of these tokens, a hero must Carry the Book to the same room as the Mummy. Any hero in the same room as both Book and the Mummy can try to defeat the Mummy in Sanity combat to complete a spell that banishes it forever.
The Mummy is immune to Speed attacks (such as those using the Revolver and Dynamite)

If You Win…
A hot, dry wind whispers through the room as you slam the ancient tome shut. The mummy shuffles toward you, its eyes dead holes of despair. As its hands close around your throat, their wrappings disintegrate. The creature moans as more and more of its body crumbles and blows away in the hot wind. “My bride… my only love… no… more… ”
As the last of the mummy disappears, the wind stops. You are alone.
